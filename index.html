01

Darb LMS

Designing a Learning Management System for course enrollment, learning management, and collaboration.

LMS UX Research User Flows Wireframes Figma

Problem

Designing a learning platform that serves trainees, instructors, and administrators can become complex when each role has different goals and workflows. The challenge was to create a structured experience that simplifies course enrollment, learning management, collaboration, and progress tracking while keeping the platform easy to navigate.

Process

The design process started with defining user roles and mapping their key journeys. User flows were created for course discovery, enrollment, assignments, projects, and administrative tasks. Wireframes and interface concepts were then developed and refined through team collaboration to ensure a consistent and scalable learning experience.

Solution & Outcome

The final solution delivers a Learning Management System that enables users to explore courses, access learning materials, collaborate through project groups, track progress, and manage educational content through role-based dashboards and administrative tools.

02

Handmade Harmony

Designing an e-commerce experience for handmade pottery products.

E-commerce Branding UX/UI User Journey Figma

Problem

Handmade products often rely on storytelling and visual presentation to build trust and encourage purchases. The challenge was to create an e-commerce experience that highlights product craftsmanship while making browsing and purchasing simple and intuitive.

Process

Research was conducted on similar handmade marketplaces and online stores to understand common user expectations. User journeys, product categorization, wireframes, and interface layouts were developed to create a clear shopping experience that supports product discovery and decision-making.

Solution & Outcome

The final design provides a clean and engaging e-commerce experience with intuitive navigation, product-focused layouts, brand storytelling elements, and a streamlined purchasing flow that improves product visibility and usability.

03

Blissed Heaven

Improving the online ordering experience for a restaurant website.

Restaurant UX/UI Information Hierarchy Wireframes Usability

Problem

Restaurant websites often overwhelm users with cluttered menus and unclear navigation, making it difficult to browse offerings and place orders efficiently. The goal was to simplify the customer journey from exploration to ordering.

Process

The existing user journey was analyzed to identify friction points in menu browsing and ordering. Wireframes and interface improvements were created to improve information hierarchy, content clarity, and overall usability.

Solution & Outcome

The redesigned experience enables users to browse menu items more efficiently, understand available options quickly, and complete their ordering journey with fewer steps and a more intuitive interface.
